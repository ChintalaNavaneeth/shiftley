# Shiftley: Financial Projections & Valuation Framework

This document provides a strategic financial overview of the Shiftley marketplace, detailing revenue streams, tax liabilities, and valuation metrics for investor discussions.

---

## 1. Revenue Model (Streams)

Shiftley operates on a **Hybrid SaaS + Marketplace** model to ensure stable recurring revenue and high-volume transaction fees.

### A. Subscription Plans (Employer SaaS)
*   **24-Hour Pass**: ₹99 (Low commitment testing)
*   **7-Day Unlimited**: ₹499 (Short-term surge hiring)
*   **30-Day Unlimited**: ₹1,499 (Standard business operations)
*   *Projected ARPU (Average Revenue Per User): ₹800 - ₹1,200 / month.*

### B. Marketplace Service Fee (Transactional)
*   **Platform Fee**: 5% charged to the Employer on every successful gig completion.
*   **Escrow Interest**: Small yields from pooled funds in Escrow (Secondary stream).

### C. Accountability Fines (Revenue Protection)
*   **No-Show Penalty**: ₹50 charged to the worker (₹25 goes to the platform, ₹25 as compensation to the employer).

## 2. Tax Implications (India)

| Tax Type | Rate | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **GST (Services)** | 18% | Applied to all Subscription and Platform fees. |
| **TDS (Section 194O)** | 1% | Deducted from worker payouts (E-commerce operator rule). |
| **Income Tax** | 25% | Corporate tax on net profit (Eligible for 3-year "Startup India" exemption). |

## 3. Profitability & Break-Even Analysis

### Unit Economics (Single Worker Lifecycle)
*   **Acquisition/Verification Cost**: ₹25 (One-time KYC)
*   **Maintenance Cost**: ₹0.50 (OTP Login)
*   **Revenue Potential**: ₹500 - ₹2,000 / month (via Platform fees and Employer subs)
*   **LTV / CAC Ratio**: Projected **20:1** (High efficiency).

### Break-Even Point
*   **Monthly Fixed Burn**: ~₹10,000 (Cloud + Communication + Misc).
*   **Subscription Break-Even**: **7 active Monthly Subscribers** covers the entire platform infrastructure.
*   **Transaction Break-Even**: **₹2 Lakhs in Monthly GMV** (Gross Merchandise Value) covers the burn.
